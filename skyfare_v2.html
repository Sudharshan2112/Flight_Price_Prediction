Saving Tips
Smart strategies to get the lowest fares on Indian routes
🗓️
Book Early
Book 6–8 weeks in advance to lock in the best domestic fares. Last-minute bookings typically cost 30–50% more.
🌙
Off-Peak Hours
Early morning (4–8 AM) and late-night departures are consistently 10–20% cheaper than prime-time flights.
📅
Avoid Peak Months
May, June, November & December see the highest fares. Travel in Jan–Mar or Aug–Sep for better deals.
🛑
Consider 1-Stop Flights
Non-stop flights are faster but 1-stop options can be up to 20% cheaper — ideal if time isn't critical.
✈️
Choose Budget Airlines
IndiGo & SpiceJet consistently offer the lowest base fares. Compare before choosing full-service carriers.
📆
Mid-Week Flights
Tuesday and Wednesday flights are typically cheaper than weekend departures by ₹300–₹800 on average.
